Research Assistant

Department of Accountancy, UIUC

Mar 2024 - May 2024

Aided advisors and students decision making with a data-driven, alumni-informed course pathway recommendation tool.

Problem Statement

  • Students arrive with diverse career aspirations—whether entering industry, launching startups, or pursuing graduate studies—and need tailored academic and extracurricular guidance.
  • The volume of course offerings and student-run clubs creates decision fatigue, making it hard for students to narrow down the options that best support their individual goals.
  • Many valuable electives and niche organizations go unnoticed, so students inadvertently miss out on opportunities that could accelerate their development.
  • As a result, “What courses should I take this semester or organizations should I join to reach my goal?” is the most common request advisors receive.

What I Did

  • Communicated with advisors and students to understand their needs.
  • Gathered data, preprocessed and anonymized it for analysis.
  • Built a recommendation tool that uses clustering to find similar alumni personas and recommend courses and organizations to students.
  • Deployed the tool using streamlit on a on-prem server.
  • Built Power BI dashboards to track cohort journey (graduation rate, first-job outcomes, salary, etc.)
